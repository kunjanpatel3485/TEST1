What is Nifty 50?

The Nifty 50 is a stock market index representing the top 50 companies listed on the National Stock Exchange (NSE) of India. It serves as one of the most important benchmarks for the performance of the Indian stock market.

The index includes companies from various sectors, such as banking, energy, IT, and consumer goods. Tracking the Nifty 50 helps investors monitor market trends and make informed investment decisions.
